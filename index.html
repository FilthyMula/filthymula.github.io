Make Tracking

  • Download the app (currently Win10/11 only - Mobile & MacOS coming soon)
  • Select "Make Tracking" and enter your details, a Tracking number will be generated automatically and stored into our DataBase
  • Choose to either email the information or save to Text/PDF File and its done

View Tracking

  • Download the app (currently Win10/11 only - Mobile & MacOS coming soon)
  • Select "View Tracking" and enter the tracking number that you have recieved
  • A new window will pop up with all the information on your item aswell as a Map Track
